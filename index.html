Krishnakumar Kunnath

Krishnakumar Kunnath, popularly known as KK, was an Indian playback singer. He recorded songs in several languages. In 2009, he won the Screen Awards, Best Playback Singer - Male for his song . He has received six Filmfare Awards nominations.On 31 May 2022, KK performed a live music concert.He complained of feeling unwell soon after his performance, where he suffered at cardiac arrest. He was 53 years old at the time of his death.
